Gymnastics

Types of gymnastics.

The three main types of gymnastics are Artistic gymnastics,Rythmic gymnastics and aerobic gymnastics. Artistic gymnastics for girls uses four apparatus,floor,beam ,vault and uneaven bars. For men there are six apparatus in artistic gymnastics,Floor Exercise Pommel Horse Still Rings ,Parallel Bars ,Vault and Horizontal bar.
